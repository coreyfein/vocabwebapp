Fill in our template, then upload it to add your words to your Vocab for Readers list.

Download Template

Only the "word" column is required -- we'll retrieve definitions, synonyms, examples, and etymology if you don't have those in your list already. If you want to use your own word data, fill in those columns as well and that's what you'll see in your list and when quizzed. Like in the template, some words might just have the word itself filled in, and others might have some or all of the other columns filled in. Files must be in .csv format to upload.
